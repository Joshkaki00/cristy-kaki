About Cristy Kaki

Holding an A.A. degree in Art, an A.S. degree in Art, and a B.A. degree in Clinical Psychology with a focus on art therapy, Cristy Kaki has been featured in books about artists in the Philippines and Canada. She grew up surrounded by various designs and patterns of shoes and slippers, as it is her family’s business. She began designing and making shoes at an early age while also learning to sing and perform cultural dances at school. This personal history gives an unusual and unique character of whimsy, innocence, and a forever striving spirit to her work.

Her vision for Art is to create calm, feel free, and connected by painting or sculpting, whenever and wherever inspiration strikes. She believes that the impact of art is building confidence, creative flexibility, and skillful thinking of all these as a gift. Different from other artists, she keeps in mind the theraputic and creative aspects of Art in her process and approach to painting and sculpting. Sometimes painting...

Beyond her artistic endeavors, Cristy is also a passionate advocate for arts education and community engagement. She regularly conducts workshops and collaborates with local organizations to promote creativity and artistic expression among young people.

Through her art, Cristy Kaki continues to push boundaries and inspire others, leaving an indelible mark on the world of contemporary art.
